Last month SFPD Police Chief Derrick Lew revealed that over the span of a year nearly 300 searches of SF's ALPR dataset were made by out-of-state and federal agencies. This story has been widely covered by [local news](https://missionlocal.org/2026/06/federal-agencies-sf-surveillance-flock-data-audit/). DeFlock SF has compiled a list of every agency that queried the city's data from  [documents](https://deflocksf.org/files/Police%20Documents/6-17-26%20NCRIC%20ALPR%20Audit%20OCR.pdf) made available by the Police Commission. Agencies listed with local divisions below often appear again without a local division (e.x. "Deputy US Marshal" without a city name).
